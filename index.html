The Basics of Personal Insurance


Personal Insurance is a type of personal insurance that you can purchase for your home, vehicle, or group. The company is a subsidiary of Desjardins General Insurance, which is the third largest P&C insurer in Canada. This article outlines some of the basics of personal insurance. We also discuss the advantages and disadvantages of each type of policy.

Personal insurance protects your net worth in case you become the target of a lawsuit. You should choose a coverage amount that reflects the value of your assets. These assets could include real estate, vehicles, investments, and valuable possessions. Experts recommend inventories of all your assets to get a fair idea of their worth.

The cost of personal insurance varies according to your location, deductibles, and the type of insurance you want to purchase. You can use an online tool to calculate the price before visiting an insurance agent. An independent agent will also know the details of your personal circumstances and can help you with any follow-up questions. The process of personal insurance is relatively simple once you understand the basics.

Personal insurance is an excellent way to protect your family against life's unexpected events. When disaster strikes, your insurance will help you and your loved ones to recover from your illness or injury. It will also provide financial security for your family in case of your death.
